tasty eats

A food based website for my Web Production II class Final Project which was heavily focused on PHP and MYSQL. Main recipes change depending on the day of the week using PHP. Viewers are able to browse around the website looking for recipes and they can give ratings/feedback and/or if they would like to contact and the information they enter will be stored using database.
